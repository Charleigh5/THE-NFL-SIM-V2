# Proposed RPG & Leveling Overhaul

This section proposes deep RPG progression systems designed to give each
position a unique developmental path. These features move beyond simple
attribute increases, offering **Unlockable Mechanics** and **Specialization
Trees**.

## Offensive Positions

### Quarterback

- **`Film Study Master`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Unlockable Ability**: Reveals one defensive coverage shell
    (Man/Zone) pre-snap after standing in the pocket for 2 seconds.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 10 + 5000 XP
  - **Positional Impact**: Drastically increases pre-snap read accuracy,
    reducing interceptions.

- **`The Architect`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Skill Tree**: Custom hot routes.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 15 + "High IQ" Trait
  - **Positional Impact**: Strategic flexibility.

### Running Back

- **`Combo Breaker`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Passive**: Jukes/spins cost 50% less stamina.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 8 + Agility > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Reduces stamina drain for elite runs.

- **`Bell Cow Certification`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Badge**: Boosts injury resistance and stamina in 4th
    quarter.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: 200 Carries/season
  - **Positional Impact**: Key for Power Back builds.

### Wide Receiver

- **`Route Artist`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Skill Tree**: Unlocks elite route animations.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 5 per route
  - **Positional Impact**: Improves receiver separation.

- **`Security Blanket`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Relationship**: +10% catch on 3rd down if QB chemistry >80.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: 3 seasons same QB
  - **Positional Impact**: Promotes QB‑WR synergy.

### Tight End

- **`Seam Buster`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Active**: +speed for 2 s on vertical release.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 12 + Speed > 80
  - **Positional Impact**: Creates vertical threat.

- **`Sixth Lineman`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Toggle**: Switch between receiver (agility) and blocker
    (strength) stance.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 5
  - **Positional Impact**: Adds tactical depth.

### Offensive Tackle

- **`Island Survivor`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Mastery**: Cancels edge rush bonus in 1‑on‑1.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 20 + Pass Block > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Improves protection flexibility.

- **`Technique Doctor`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Mentorship**: +5 Pass Block to adjacent guard.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 10 Veteran (>30yo)
  - **Positional Impact**: Helps rookie guards.

### Offensive Guard

- **`Pull Train`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Physics**: +20% impact when pulling.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 8 + Strength > 85
  - **Positional Impact**: Boosts power‑run blocking.

- **`Pocket Anchor`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Upgrade**: Resists Bull Rush beyond 1 yd.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 12
  - **Positional Impact**: Maintains pocket integrity.

### Center

- **`Line General`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Pre‑Snap**: Detects "Mike" LB, resets line assignments.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 15 + Awareness > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Counters blitz confusion.

- **`Snap Perfection`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Passive**: No snap fumbles, faster snap.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 5
  - **Positional Impact**: Helps shotgun timing.

## Defensive Positions

### Defensive End

- **`Edge Threat`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Stance Modifier**: Can choose a "Wide 9" alignment to
    sacrifice run defense for a massive speed rush bonus.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 10
  - **Positional Impact**: Forces the offense to chip with a RB or keep a TE in
    to block.

- **`Spin Cycle`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Animation Unlock**: Unlocks a "Elite Spin Move" that is
    twice as fast as the default.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 8 + Finesse > 85
  - **Positional Impact**: Gives speed rushers a reliable counter to heavy, slow
    Offensive Tackles.

### Defensive Tackle

- **`Grave Digger`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Physics**: Expands "Pile Up" radius, blocks two gaps.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 12 + Weight > 320lbs
  - **Positional Impact**: Frees LBs.

- **`Interior Disruptor`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Passive**: 15% chance to shed block on QB pressure.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 15
  - **Positional Impact**: Adds interior pressure.

### Linebacker

- **`Field Commander`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Vision**: Expands defense fog of war.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 18 + Awareness > 95
  - **Positional Impact**: Prevents surprise formations.

- **`Lurker`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Jump**: Super jumps to intercept 5‑10 yd passes.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 10 + Jump > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Stops intermediate routes.

### Cornerback

- **`Island King`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Isolation**: +5 stats without safety help.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 20 + Man Cov > 95
  - **Positional Impact**: Enables aggressive blitzing.

- **`Route Jumper`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Predict**: 50% bonus on 4th repeat route.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 12 + Play Rec > 85
  - **Positional Impact**: Discourages route repetition.

### Safety

- **`Hit Stick Master`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Physics**: Tackles 20% chance to cause incompletion.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 8 + Hit Power > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Increases turnover risk.

- **`Robber Role`**
  - **Mechanism**: **AI**: Adds "Robber" zone to trap crossing routes.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 14
  - **Positional Impact**: Confuses QBs.

## Special Teams Positions

### Kicker

- **`Clutch Kicker`**

  - **Mechanism**: **Meter**: Accuracy zone unchanged in 4Q/OT.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 10 + "Ice in Veins"
  - **Positional Impact**: Improves clutch kicks.

- **`Calibration`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Prep**: Warm‑up boosts accuracy 5% for next drive.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 5
  - **Positional Impact**: Adds sideline mini‑game.

### Punter

- **`Pin Point`**

  - **Mechanism**: **UI**: Shows trajectory for "Coffin Corner" punts.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 12 + Acc > 90
  - **Positional Impact**: Improves field position.

- **`Fake Specialist`**
  - **Mechanism**: **Skill**: Boosts pass/run for fake punts.
  - **XP Cost / Requirement**: Level 8
  - **Positional Impact**: Forces "Punt Safe" defense.
