# Special Teams

## Kicker (K) & Punter (P)

### Key Attributes

- **`kick_power`**

  - Max distance.

- **`kick_accuracy`**
  - Probability of straight flight.

## Special Teams Enhancements

### Kicker / Punter

- **`Ice in Veins`** (Trait)

  - **Description**: Negates the "icing the kicker" mechanic (shaking meter/camera).
  - **Impact**: **Reliability**: Guarantees points in clutch situations, taking pressure off the **Offense** to score TDs.

- **`Hang Time`** (Attribute)

  - **Description**: Determines time ball is in air; affects coverage team arrival time.
  - **Impact**: **Coverage Aid**: High hang time allows **Gunners** to reach the returner before the catch, preventing big returns.

- **`Coffin Corner`** (Attribute)

  - **Description**: Accuracy specifically for punts landing inside the 20-yard line.
  - **Impact**: **Field Position**: Pins the opponent deep, drastically increasing the **Defense's** chance of scoring a Safety or forcing a 3-and-out.

- **`Return Vision`** (Attribute)
  - **Description**: (For Returners) AI ability to spot lanes and set up blocks.
  - **Impact**: **Flip the Field**: A good return gives the **Offense** a short field, increasing scoring probability.

### Gunner

- **`Vice`** (Role)
  - **Description**: (Gunner Specific) Ability to beat double-team blocks on the outside.
  - **Impact**: **Forces Fair Catch**: Disrupts the **Return Team's** blocking scheme, forcing the Returner to signal fair catch.
